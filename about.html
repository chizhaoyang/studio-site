About the Studio

Crescendo Journey Music Studio was founded by Minghao Liu in 2025 with a single goal: to create a patient, resilient, and inclusive space where students of all backgrounds can discover the joy of music. Whether preparing for ABRSM exams or learning their very first notes, every student follows a personalized journey that values steady growth over speed.

About the Founder

Minghao Liu, M.M.

• Master of Music, Berklee College of Music
• Master of Music, West Virginia University
• Bachelor of Piano Performance, Sichuan Conservatory

With more than ten years of piano-teaching experience, Minghao prepares students for ABRSM examinations, AP Music Theory, and a lifetime of joyful playing. His teaching blends patience with creativity, fostering resilience and musical curiosity in every learner.
